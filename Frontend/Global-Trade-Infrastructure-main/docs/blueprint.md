# **App Name**: Baalvion

## Core Features:

- Trade Management: Create, negotiate, and approve trades with multi-party approval workflows and role-specific views.
- Finance & Settlement: Manage FX rates, multi-currency support, and settlement tracking with alerts for failed settlements or currency fluctuations.
- Compliance & Regulatory: Perform KYC, AML, and sanction screening with automated document verification and compliance alerts.
- Logistics & Shipment: Track shipments, milestones, delays, and disruptions with carrier integration and insurance options.
- AI-Assisted Risk Intelligence: Provide vendor/counterparty scoring, predictive analytics for defaults/disruptions, and AI risk alerts for trade corridors using a reputation decay tool.
- Crisis & Emergency Management: Handle trade freezes, dispute resolution, and what-if scenario simulations with emergency alerts and audit trail logging.
- Admin/Governance: Super Admin full control, role-based access, permission management, and emergency kill switches.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep ocean blue (#2962FF) to convey trust and stability.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F4F8) for a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Bright cyan (#00B8D4) for interactive elements and key notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern, machined, objective, neutral look that suits both headlines and body text.
- Use consistent, professional icons from a library like FontAwesome or Material Design to represent different trade functions.
- Dashboard layouts should be modular and responsive, with clear separation of concerns for each role (importer, exporter, admin, etc.).
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
