# **App Name**: GeoTrade Nexus

## Core Features:

- User and Company Onboarding & Management: Allows B2B users (Sellers, Buyers, Logistics, etc.) to register, create and manage company profiles, roles, and verify mining licenses for compliance. Includes 2FA and role-based access control setup.
- Product Listing and Inventory Management: Sellers can create, edit, and publish detailed product listings for various minerals, including grade, purity, quantity, pricing, origin, and certifications. Manages available inventory in real-time.
- Marketplace Browsing and Advanced Search: Buyers can efficiently search, filter, and compare product listings based on mineral type, grade, location, certifications, and other specifications.
- Request For Quotation (RFQ) System: Facilitates the creation and management of RFQs, allowing buyers to solicit bids for specific material requirements and sellers to submit proposals.
- Comprehensive Order and Contract Management: Manages direct purchases, bulk orders, long-term supply contracts, and supports auction/tender bidding processes from creation to fulfillment.
- Integrated Logistics and Shipment Tracking: Enables booking and management of various transport types (truck, rail, sea, container), freight rate calculation, and real-time shipment tracking with status updates.
- Warehouse Operations and Inventory Control: Supports warehouse registration, detailed inventory tracking, inbound/outbound shipment management, stock alerts, and quality inspection records.
- Secure Payment and Escrow System: Integrates multiple payment methods (Stripe, PayPal, Bank Transfer, LC) with escrow protection to ensure secure and transparent financial transactions.
- Dispute Resolution and Ratings & Reviews: Provides a structured process for resolving trade disputes and allows users to submit ratings and reviews for products, sellers, and service providers.
- In-Platform Messaging and Notification System: Facilitates direct communication between all user roles and delivers real-time notifications for critical events, order updates, and system alerts.
- Role-Based Analytics and Admin Dashboards: Offers tailored dashboards for each user role (Admin, Seller, Buyer, Logistics, Warehouse, Finance, Moderator, Support) with integrated analytics, notifications, and messaging, ensuring role-based access control.
- AI-Powered Product Description Tool: Assists sellers in generating concise and standardized product descriptions based on input material specifications, using a tool with reasoning capabilities to create relevant and accurate text.
- AI-Powered Compliance Verification Tool: Utilizes an AI tool to assist in verifying mining licenses, product certifications, and quality inspection reports, ensuring regulatory adherence and trust by reasoning about submitted documents.
- AI-Powered Market Intelligence Tool: Provides insights into market trends, pricing fluctuations, and demand forecasting for various minerals, leveraging an AI tool to analyze extensive market data and provide actionable recommendations.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to promote clarity and readability for detailed data and long-form content. Primary brand color: A deep, professional blue (#1B4498) to signify trust and global reach, ideal for accents, buttons, and important textual elements. Background color: A very light, subtle grey-blue (#ECF0F5) providing a clean canvas. Accent color: A vibrant aqua (#21CEDD) for highlighting calls to action and interactive elements, providing a modern contrast.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities across various screen sizes, ensuring clear presentation of complex information and data.
- Use a set of clear, professional, and minimalist outline icons for navigation, feature actions, and status indicators, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic suitable for a B2B platform.
- Employ a structured, grid-based layout with clear data presentation. Utilize card-based designs for dashboards and listings to organize information efficiently, ensuring easy navigation and comprehension of details like product specifications and order status.
- Implement subtle and purposeful animations for user feedback, such as on button clicks, form submissions, and data loading states, enhancing user experience without distracting from the platform's core functionality.
